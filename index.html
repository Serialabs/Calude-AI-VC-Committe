How It Works

1

Deal Memo Extraction

AI analyzes the pitch deck and extracts key information: problem, solution, business model, market size, traction, and competitive landscape.

2

VC Evaluation

Ten AI-simulated venture capitalists independently evaluate the startup, each bringing unique expertise and investment perspectives.

3

Committee Debate

Three selected VCs engage in a structured multi-round debate as Bull, Bear, and Wild Card, arguing the investment merits.

4

Final Report

A comprehensive report with key insights, founder questions, action plans, and an executive summary with the committee's recommendation.

AIVC Prompt — System Instructions

The simulation is powered by a carefully crafted prompt that defines 20 VC personas, a structured debate format, and a comprehensive evaluation framework. Every report follows the same rigorous process.

About This Project

The AI-Powered VC Investment Committee is an experiment in using large language models to simulate the venture capital evaluation process. By analyzing historical pitch decks through the eyes of 20 distinct AI-simulated VC personas, we explore how today's AI would assess yesterday's most iconic startups.

This is a simulation for educational and entertainment purposes. No real investment decisions are represented.
