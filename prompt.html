Discover the system instructions that power the AI Investment Committee — 20 unique VC personas, a structured debate format, and a rigorous evaluation framework.

The Four-Stage Pipeline

Every pitch deck goes through the same rigorous evaluation process.

1

Deal Memo Extraction

Parse the pitch deck into structured data: problem, solution, business model, market, traction, competition, and potential outcomes.

2

Independent Evaluation

Ten VC personas independently evaluate the startup. Each identifies strengths, concerns, and a unique perspective before casting their vote.

3

Committee Debate

Three VCs are selected as Bull, Bear, and Wild Card. They debate across five rounds: initial positions, cross-examination, new information, refinement, and consensus.

4

Final Report

A comprehensive output including key insights, questions for the founder, action plans, and an executive summary with the committee's final recommendation.

Debate Structure

The investment committee debate unfolds across five structured rounds:

  • Round 1 — Initial Positions: Each panelist states their investment thesis
  • Round 2 — Cross-Examination: Panelists challenge each other's assumptions
  • Round 3 — New Information: Additional data points and market context are introduced
  • Round 4 — Position Refinement: Panelists update their views based on the discussion
  • Round 5 — Final Consensus: The committee reaches (or fails to reach) a collective decision

Roles: The Bull is the strongest advocate, the Bear is the most skeptical, and the Wild Card brings an unexpected perspective.
