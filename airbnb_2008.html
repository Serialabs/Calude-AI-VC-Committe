Seed 2008 Hospitality

AirBed&Breakfast

Peer-to-peer home rental marketplace

Stage 1: Deal Memo Extraction

Problem
  • Hotels are expensive especially during events
  • Travelers lack authentic local experiences
  • No way to monetize spare rooms
Solution
  • Peer-to-peer home rental marketplace
  • Air mattress to entire homes
  • Photo-verified listings
  • Review system
Business Model
  • 10% commission (3% host, 6-12% guest)
  • No inventory owned
  • Marketplace model
Stage
  • Seed round
  • Launched during SXSW and DNC
  • Very early stage
Market Size
  • US hotel industry $150B+
  • Global hospitality $500B+
  • Vacation rental segment growing
Traction & Ask
  • Very little — a few hundred bookings total
  • Primarily event-driven spikes
Potential Outcomes
  • Bull: Global hospitality disruptor ($30B+)
  • Bear: Trust/safety issues kill the concept, regulatory shutdown
Competition
  • Hotels
  • VRBO
  • Craigslist short-term rentals
  • Couchsurfing (free)

Debate Triggers

  • Trust and safety issues (strangers in homes)
  • Very little traction
  • Unrealistic market projections
  • Recession timing could help or hurt
  • Regulatory uncertainty in cities
